Year-End Processing Information

Leave Carry Forward Rules

  • Privileged Leave: For Regular Officers and Executives, unused days up to the maximum limit can be carried forward until March 31st of the next year.
  • Casual Leave: Expires on December 31st each year, no carry forward.
  • Sick Leave: For Regular employees, unused days are frozen yearly and accumulate. For Contractual employees, expires at the end of the contract period.

Minimum Consumption Requirements

  • Regular Officers: Must consume at least 15 days of Privileged Leave for encashment eligibility.
  • Contractual Staff: Must use at least 15 Privileged Leave days yearly.

Leave Period Variations

  • Regular Officers: Leave period typically starts January 1st.
  • Regular Executives: Privileged Leave period ends March 31st of the next year.
  • Contractual Staff: Leave period based on contract start date.
